  • 1. what is Nebulas Piggy?（星云小猪是什么？）
    Nebulas Piggy is an simple and useful piggy bank, that you can save and withdraw Nas according the plan you have made.
    （星云小猪是一个简单好用的小猪存钱罐，你可以安州你的计划存取Nas。)

  • 2. How to use it？（怎么使用？）

    Create My Piggy, fill in the infromation of your piggy and click this button and send the transaction successfully, then you can create your own piggy bank.
    点击创建地址对应的小猪存钱罐，需要填写目标存款数，取款日期，以及描述，发送交易成功后即已成功创建。

    Fill in your wallet address so you can view & check your piggy bank information.
    点击输入自己的钱包地址，即可查看对应的小猪存钱罐信息。（如已存入数目等）

    Fill in the number of nas which you want to deposit, send the transaction successfully, then your piggy bank's deposit will update.
    点击输入要存入的金额数（Nas),发送交易并成功后即表明已存入，也可以通过上一步查看信息。

    When you reach any of your goal (target amount of deposit or withdraw date), you can click this and send the transaction successfully ,then your deposit will be withdrawed.
    取款，当达成任意的目标（如目标存款数或取款日期） 即可取款，只收取交易的gas费用。

    When you don't reach any of your goal,you can also withdraw your deposit Nas, but it's will cost 5% of the deposit!
    提前取款，当还没有达成任何目标但需要取款情形，但需要耗费5%的存款金额，请认真对待自己的计划哦。
